This Halloween, I have an ambitious agenda! First, I plan to gather my friends for a classic nighttime adventure of egging houses. I’ll don a creative costume, ready to charm unsuspecting homeowners and fill my bag with an abundance of candy. There’s something especially delightful about collecting sweets from elementary-aged children—it's all in good fun, of course! Afterward, the real indulgence begins as I settle down to enjoy the candy haul. I’ll savor each piece, relishing the sugary flavors and memories of childhood Halloweens past. But my mischief doesn’t stop there! To add a truly festive twist, I’ll play my newly acquired pipe organ, which I’ll have “borrowed” from a local establishment. Imagine the hilarity and chaos as I serenade my neighbors with haunting melodies that echo through the night, creating a perfect mix of delight and confusion. Hehe~ What a night it will be, filled with laughter, mischief, and a dash of spooky fun!
